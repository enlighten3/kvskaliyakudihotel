KVS Kaliyakudi Hotel & Caters


Mayavaram Kaliyakudyi hotal was started in the year - 1932 at Mayiladuthurai [the then Mayavaram town], a hindu pilgrim center famous for 'Kadamulzhuku' [a divine dip in the river ofKaveri to relinquish the sins ofthe human birth]


Sri.K.V.SRINIVASA IYER was the founder of the pioneer restaurant of this kind which is famous for ALWA, RAVA DOSAI and South Indian filter coffee.


The Hindu generation of the restaurant has grown and spread all over Tamil Nadu with tradition and taste of vegetarian.
